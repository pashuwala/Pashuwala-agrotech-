Your 7-Day Refund Guarantee

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, you may request a refund within 7 calendar days from the date of delivery. We review every case fairly and promptly.

1

Overview

At Pashuwala Agrotech, customer satisfaction is our priority. We have established a fair and transparent 7-Day Refund Policy to ensure you can shop with confidence. This policy applies to all online purchases made through our website.

2

Refund Eligibility — 7-Day Window

You are eligible to request a refund within 7 (seven) calendar days from the date of delivery, provided:

  • The product delivered is defective, damaged, or significantly different from the website description.
  • The product was not delivered at all (non-delivery claim).
  • Incorrect product or wrong quantity was delivered.
  • The refund request is raised within 7 days with valid supporting evidence.
❌ Refund requests raised after 7 days from the delivery date will NOT be entertained under any circumstances.
3

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • The refund request is made after 7 days of delivery.
  • The product has been used, partially consumed, or altered.
  • Damage caused by improper storage, handling, or use by the buyer.
  • Change of mind or buyer's remorse after purchase.
  • Products purchased during special promotions or bulk/wholesale orders (unless specified).
  • Minor variation in appearance or weight within acceptable tolerance for agricultural goods.
4

How to Request a Refund

Follow these steps to raise your refund request:

1

Raise a Request

Contact us within 7 days of delivery via email or the website contact form. Include your Order ID, delivery date, and a clear description of the issue.

2

Submit Evidence

Share clear photographs or a short video of the defective / damaged / incorrect product as proof of your claim.

3

Review by Our Team

Our team will review your request and evidence within 2–3 business days and notify you of the decision via email.

4

Product Return (if applicable)

If instructed, arrange to send the product back in its original packaging. Return shipping charges may apply unless the issue is on our end.

5

Refund Processed ✓

Upon approval and verification, the refund will be initiated within 7–10 business days to your original payment method.

6

Replacement Option

In certain eligible cases, at our discretion, we may offer a product replacement instead of a monetary refund. This will be communicated to you during the refund review process.

7

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled before dispatch. Once the product has been dispatched, cancellation is not possible.
  • To cancel an order, contact our support team immediately with your Order ID.
  • For cancelled (pre-dispatch) orders, a full refund will be processed within 7–10 business days.
8

Contact for Refund Queries

For all refund-related queries, please contact Pashuwala Agrotech through the contact details or support section on our website. Please keep your Order ID and delivery details handy for a faster resolution.
