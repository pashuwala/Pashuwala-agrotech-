What Makes Our Silage Special?

🔬
Controlled Fermentation
Green fodder is compacted and wrapped airtight in white stretch film. Inside, natural fermentation produces lactic acid that preserves all nutrients perfectly for months.
🌡️
Nutrient-Locked All Year
Unlike dry fodder, silage retains moisture, digestible fiber, vitamins, and energy exactly as harvested — no nutrient loss even after 12 months of storage.
🐄
Highly Palatable
The tangy, fermented aroma is irresistible to cattle. Better feed intake = better growth, higher milk yield, and improved reproductive performance.
💰
Economical Bulk Supply
Harvest in bulk during peak season and store as silage bales. Eliminates fodder scarcity in summer & winter — the most cost-effective feeding strategy.
